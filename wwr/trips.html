Trip 1: Canyon Rapids Adventure

Experience the thrill of whitewater rafting through the scenic Canyon Rapids! This half-day trip is perfect for beginners and families who want to enjoy a fun, splash-filled adventure surrounded by breathtaking mountain views. Our certified guides will teach you the basics of paddling and keep the energy high as you ride through gentle rapids and calm stretches of river. It’s the ideal mix of excitement and natural beauty, no prior experience required!

Trip 2: Wilderness Expedition

Ready for a real adventure? The Wilderness Expedition takes you deep into the untamed river valleys for a two-day rafting journey you’ll never forget. You’ll paddle through Class III–IV rapids, camp overnight under the stars, and enjoy meals prepared by your guides right by the river. Perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers who want a complete rafting experience.

Trip 3: Sunset River Run

End your day with a splash! The Sunset River Run is a full-day adventure that concludes with a breathtaking golden-hour paddle.As you navigate moderate rapids and calm stretches, you’ll enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding cliffs glowing in the sunset light.

Trip 4: Extreme Rapids Challenge

For experienced rafters seeking the ultimate test of skill and endurance, the Extreme Rapids Challenge delivers nonstop excitement. This three-day expedition takes you through the wildest sections of the river, featuring Class IV–V rapids, steep drops, and technical maneuvers.
