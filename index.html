Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic is a Caribbean nation located on the eastern side of Hispaniola, part of the Greater Antilles archipelago. It shares its only land border with Haiti to the west and is separated from Puerto Rico by the Mona Passage to the east. Covering about 48,671 square kilometers (18,792 sq mi), the country is the second-largest in the Antilles by both size and population. In 2024, its population was estimated at 11.4 million people, with roughly 3.6 million residing in the capital and largest city, Santo Domingo.
